Structural Tags (HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER)

Structural tags are elements used to divide a website into primary sections.

What goes in the header?

It defines a header for the website. There can be major things such as logo.

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

The Nav is used to gather a set of navigational links. It can be placed in the header, but it doesn't have to go in the header. It can be anywhere on the webpage where it needed. Typically the Nav is used for navigating within my own website. It also can contain links to another website, but it depends on.

What goes in Main?

It has the main contents of the page that is related to its purpose. For examples, articles, posts, or key information.

What goes in the Footer?

It is used for content at the bottom of a section or a page. Typically it includes information about the websites or authors.
