Top 3 Destinations in Korea

Seoul

Explore the vibrant city of Seoul, blending tradition and modernity.

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace – Iconic royal palace with guard changing ceremonies.
  • Myeongdong – A paradise for shoppers and foodies alike.
  • N Seoul Tower – Enjoy a panoramic view of the city skyline.
  • Hongdae – Youthful district filled with art, music, and nightlife.

Jeju Island

Jeju is a volcanic island filled with stunning landscapes and unique culture.

  • Hallasan Mountain – Korea’s highest peak, ideal for hiking lovers.
  • Manjanggul Cave – A UNESCO lava tube with incredible formations.
  • Seongsan Ilchulbong – A dramatic tuff cone perfect for sunrise views.
  • Jeongbang Waterfall – One of the few waterfalls in Asia that falls directly into the ocean.

Busan

Busan is a coastal city known for its relaxed atmosphere, beaches, and seafood. Must-see attractions include:

  • Haeundae Beach – A lively beach with cafes and events.
  • Gamcheon Culture Village – Colorful houses, murals, and quirky shops.
  • Jagalchi Fish Market – Try fresh seafood and witness local fishmongers in action.
  • Beomeosa Temple – A serene temple nestled in the mountains.
