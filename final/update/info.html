Useful Travel Info About Korea

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April–June) and Autumn (September–November) are the best seasons to visit Korea. The weather is mild, and you can enjoy cherry blossoms or colorful fall leaves.

Top Attractions

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace in Seoul
  • Jeju Island
  • Bukchon Hanok Village
  • N Seoul Tower

Transportation

Korea has an excellent public transportation system. You can easily travel by subway, bus, or KTX (high-speed train). Get a T-money card to pay for most transport easily.

Food & Culture

Try local dishes like kimchi, bulgogi, and bibimbap. Meals are often shared, and side dishes (banchan) are free and refillable!

Etiquette Tips

  • Take off your shoes when entering a home.
  • Use both hands when giving or receiving something from an elder.
  • Don’t stick chopsticks straight up in your rice bowl.
Source: Korean Cultural Center New York
