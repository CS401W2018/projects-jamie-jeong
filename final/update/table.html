Top 5 Travel Tips for Visiting Korea

  1. Get a T-money Card: Use this rechargeable card for public transportation and even convenience stores across Korea. It’s super convenient!
  2. Download Essential Apps: Apps like Naver Map, Papago (translator), and Kakao Metro will save you tons of time.
  3. Learn a Few Korean Phrases: Basic greetings and phrases like “Thank you” (감사합니다) go a long way in connecting with locals.
  4. Try Street Food: Don’t miss out on tteokbokki, hotteok, and odeng at local markets like Gwangjang or Myeongdong.
  5. Respect Cultural Norms: Take your shoes off indoors, use both hands when giving/receiving items, and bow slightly when greeting.

By following these tips, you’ll have a smoother and more enjoyable experience in Korea!
