Explore South Korea: 5 Iconic Destinations

South Korea has so many amazing places to visit, from historic sites to stunning natural landscapes. This tour will take you through five unique spots, each with its own charm. Whether you are into history, city views, or nature, there is something for everyone to enjoy!

  • Seoul-Gyeongbokgung Palace
  • Busan-Haeundae Beach & Gwangan Bridge
  • Gyeongju-Bulguksa Temple & Cheomseongdae
  • Jeju Island-Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak)
  • Gangwon Province-Seoraksan National Park
