Elasti-girl – Helen Parr

Helen Parsons is a superhero with a very flexible body. She is in charge of protecting her family and fighting crime. Helen is very smart and always tries to solve problems calmly. Her threat rating is 6.2.

Mr. Incredible – Robert "Bob" Parr

Bob Parr is a superhero, and he's very strong. He's always trying to protect his family, and he's saved a lot of people in the past. He's a regular guy now, but he still takes pride in being a superhero. His threat rating is 9.1.

Violet "Vi" Parr

Violet has the ability to hide herself. She can become invisible, she can create a shield. She's an introvert, but she loves her family, and she's getting stronger and stronger.

Dashiell Robert Parr

Dash is a boy with very high speed. He can run much faster than others. Dash is always full of energy, and sometimes he hides his abilities.

John Jackson Parr

Jack-Jack is still a baby, but he has amazing powers. He can do a lot of different things, like breathe fire and shapeshift into different forms. He can't control them yet, but he's very special.
