What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags take up the full width of a line, and always start on a new line. They have height and width. Inline tags surround text, only take up as much space as needed, and stay on the same line.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is space outside the box, it moves the entire box away from other elements. Border is the edge of the box, it adds a visible line around the element. Padding is space inside the box, and the content moves inward, creates space inside the border.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

For borders, we can use px, thin, in, cm and they are fixed-size measurement. As relative measurement, we have % and em. Em unit is a relative measurement based on the font size of its parent element.

When would you use a figure tag?

A figure tag can be used for images with captions, grouping images. It is often used with the FIGCAPTION tag to provide a caption for the content inside.
