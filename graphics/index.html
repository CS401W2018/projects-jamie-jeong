What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

Photos with lots of details and colors are good for using JPEG. Good with real life photo. Losing some information if it saves in small size.

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

Photos with few simple designs and colors is good for using GIF. Good for cartoon-like pictures, and animation.

What is unique about the PNG format?

Unlike JPEG, PNG supports transparency. Also, it has the better quality than GIF and doesn't lose quality when saved. Good for cartoon, logos, and alpha.

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

  • Adjust width and height in CSS.
  • Use editing function in the Mac or photo editing software.

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

  • Larger images take longer to load even if CSS makes them look smaller.
  • Resizing in CSS can make images look pixelated and blurry.
