What is normal flow in HTML?

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float allows text or other content to wrap around it by moving an element to the left or right. Float can make elements to affect the layout of other elements. Clear: both can be used to make the next element is positioned correctly.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

  • Absolute fixes the position of an element relative to its parent element.
  • Fixed makes the element to stau in the same place on the screen even when scrolling.
  • Relative makes the element to move relative to its normal position.
